## About

**Lalu Graphics** is not just a documentation site; it is a "Visual Sovereignty" roadmap. It serves as a comprehensive guide covering everything from the 7 Immutable Laws of Design to advanced 2026 AI workflows.

**Key Features:**
- **Interactive Visualizers:** Real-time demos for Golden Ratio, Typography Anatomy, and Color Theory (60-30-10 Rule).
- **Localized Context:** Specific guides on pairing Google Noto Sans Ethiopic with modern sans-serifs.
- **Future-Proofing:** A dedicated module on integrating AI (Flux, Midjourney) into professional workflows without losing the human touch.
